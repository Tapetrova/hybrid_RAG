Hybrid RAG: Adaptive Hallucination Suppression in Retrieval-Augmented Generation

Domain-Specific Q&A for Automotive Systems

Abstract

We present Hybrid-AHS, a novel approach for reducing hallucinations in automotive domain Q&A systems through adaptive combination of vector and graph-based retrieval methods. Our method achieves 89.6% weighted factual accuracy with the highest robustness across question categories (RAC = 0.942).
